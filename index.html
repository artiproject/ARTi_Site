ARTI_Token

Arti Token is the main payment method used within the art project ecosystem.

The ARTI token used in the ARTI project is a utility token issued by CREATIVE IDEA LIMITED in Hong Kong for Non Fungible Token (NFT) global payments under the motto of creative thinking and art.

ARTI tokens will be used as a major payment method within the foundation ecosystem consisting of companies and organizations that promote ARTI PROJECT in Korea. The major companies involved in the Arti Project Foundation are mainly used in small and medium-sized galleries, art exchanges, NFT exchanges, ancient coin galleries, characters and items from many game companies, and digital arts, focusing on the Hooranky Foundation and Onggi Art Group.

ARTI token is used as a means of co-ownership, sale, auction, exhibition, donation, and payment for artworks and digital contents of famous artists abroad as well as for various NFT-enabled game items, characters, and celebrity goods.

BACKGROUND

Transforming the Art Market into NFT Digitalization.

The ARTI Project improves the problem of the art market in the digital age. We want to realize digitalization and decentralization of ownership, transparency of transactions, liquidity of ownership, and popularization of art.

The reality of the art market in the digital age.

Today's industrial environment is the era of the Fourth Industrial Revolution, which is well known for artificial intelligence and big data in the Internet revolution. However, in the field of art and art, it is still very closed, alienated from changes in the digital information revolution, such as the opacity of transactions and information asymmetry.

Problems in the art trade market.

Because it is difficult for the general public to access and expensive artworks are more difficult for the general public to own or trade, artworks are considered exclusive to the wealthy and there are bad perceptions such as illegal trade and tax evasion.

The advent of Internet and blockchain technology.

As the Internet and digital transformation speeds up, time and space constraints have enabled previously impossible transactions, and blockchain blockchain (BLOCKCHAIN) technologies and services to support digital markets, payments and security that enable them. People around the world can own a variety of assets in part, share and use them together, tokenize them as easily as a single item, and now they are able to trade NFT transactions that only trade in real as well as ownership, generating financial returns.

Digitalization Revolution of Art Using NFT Technology

To improve the existing chronic problems of this art market and digital content (music, web tunes, movies, game items) market, the art project seeks to transparentize distribution processes by recording assets on blockchain-based non-fungible tokens.

PROJECT

Main ARTI Service 01

01.ARTI-Blockchain

Art history management and forgery prevention services

On Arti Project's platform, you can use blockchain technology to give unique values and store information about the entire distribution as encrypted Hash values on blockchain servers to permanently and invariably without falsification. In the art market, counterfeiting, imitation, reproduction, and falsification have continued to occur, especially in the distribution process, and it is used as a means of tax evasion, crime funds, and property concealment, causing the public to lose awareness and confidence in the art trade.

Main ARTI Service 02

02.ARTI-NFT Certification

NFT Registration and Certification Services

Arti Project introduces digitalization of artworks and registration service for NFT works along with "Korea NFT Certified Authority". In the meantime, there has been a problem of how blockchain technology combines with art, and Arti uses the "patent application for art storage and digitization" technology of Korea NFT Certificate Authority to digitalize art and register and certify ownership.

The artist certifies himself as authentic, permanently records it on the blockchain "public digital ledger", contains information on all works, including the name of the work, transaction history, and winning bid price, and information that determines the authenticity of the work through spectroscopy. Payment required for blockchain registration and authentication services through NFTization can be made with an art token.

Main ARTI Service 03

03.ARTI-X /eXchange

NFT exchanges and marketplaces

Arti Project establishes a trading platform where anyone can purchase, sell, or co-purchase art. Designed and built to tokenize all digital content into NFTs. Through this, anyone in the public can become an art owner with a small amount of money and generate profits through transactions.

Main ARTI Service 04

04.ARTI-Filter

NFT Works Filtering Certification System

In order to enter the art platform, security technologies that are different from ordinary e-commerce are required, and creators, artists, and collectors must go through real-name authentication and anti-money laundering procedures.

Arti Platform reviews and certifies the authenticity of works through NFT certification institutions and experts. As reckless items that are not verified in the current NFT market come up, quality deterioration can be eliminated in advance. Certified artworks issue unique tokens with irreplaceable hash values by applying NFT technology to prevent forgery of ownership, and irreplaceable unique tokens are registered and stored in the blockchain. Within the Arti Marketplace, the payment method is centered around the Arti token.

Main ARTI Service 05

05.ARTI-NFT Wallet

NFT Token Wallet

Developed based on ERC-20, ERC-721, and ERC-1155, Arti Wallet has two main functions: purchasing for NFT purchases and wallet for NFT asset management.

The 'NFT Crosschain Wallet' is available, allowing all blockchain-based NFTs in the world to be managed in one place. To achieve this, Arti Wellet integrates with several decentralized exchanges to promote high liquidity in NFT tokens. Arti tokens will complement the arti ecosystem by creating a sustainable token economy, and achieve transaction finality in the safest and most transparent way.
